# ❓ FAQ

---

**Q: Is this the actual source code?**
No. This repo is an open showcase — documentation, API guides, and our public roadmap. The app runs on private infrastructure.

**Q: Is the API really free?**
Yes, for basic queries. No API key needed. Premium/bulk access uses x402 micropayments — you pay per-request in crypto, no subscription.

**Q: How do I claim my business?**
Go to [support-local-businesses.com](https://support-local-businesses.com), search for your business, and click "Claim Your Listing." You'll get a magic link via email.

**Q: Why would I claim my listing?**
Free visibility report, blog post, podcast episode, original song, promo video, gamification perks, coupon tracking, and a managed profile that shows up in AI agent searches.

**Q: Wait — you'll make a SONG about my business?**
Yes. An actual AI-composed song with lyrics about your specific business. It's fun, shareable, and nobody else does this.

**Q: How accurate is the data?**
Base data comes from FL government records (very accurate for licensed businesses). Enrichment data (websites, phones) comes from Google Places, Yelp, and BBB — generally reliable but we can't guarantee 100% accuracy. Business owners can correct anything by claiming their listing.

**Q: What's x402?**
A protocol for machine-to-machine payments. Your AI agent pays our API in crypto (Base L2) per-request. No API keys, no accounts, no subscriptions. [Learn more](https://x402.org).

**Q: What's MCP?**
Model Context Protocol — a standard for AI tools. Add our MCP server to Claude, Cursor, or Windsurf and your AI can search our directory conversationally.

**Q: Only 5 claimed listings? Is this real?**
Very real. We have 6.4M listings and just launched the claim feature. The low claim rate is our biggest opportunity — and we're being transparent about it.

**Q: Can I contribute data?**
Yes! See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md). Open an issue if you have a clean dataset.

**Q: Who built this?**
One human founder + AI co-founders, running on [Polsia](https://polsia.com). No VC funding. Self-sustaining by design.

**Q: How do I contact you?**
Use the chatbot on any page at [support-local-businesses.com](https://support-local-businesses.com), or open an issue in this repo.
